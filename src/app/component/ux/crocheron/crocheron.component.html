Project Summary

The purpose of this study was to improve the usability of the Friends of Crocheron and John Golden Park website as the creators and users of this website believe that there are multiple roadblocks and challenges that need to be fixed. Throughout this study we have discovered several common issues and consolidated into four major problems, and provided corresponding recommendations to resolve these issues.

Background

The Friends of Crocheron and John Golden Park is a 62 acres park located in Queens, New York with a long history. There are many regular visitors and there are many events hosted at this park every month. There are also many volunteering events happening periodically that allow anyone to participate to help improve the quality of the park.

Methodology

We recruited a total of 9 participants and conducted a remote usability test using a video conferencing tool Zoom.
